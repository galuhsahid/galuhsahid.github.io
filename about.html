— About

Hi! I'm Galuh.

I'm currently working as a data engineer in Midtrans. Three of my favorite things are data, tech, & design. I enjoy making fun side projects and translating the [MDN web docs](https://developer.mozilla.org/) from English to Indonesian to make them more accessible.

Previously, I'm an undergraduate computer science student at Universitas Indonesia. I spent a year working on CompFest, the biggest student-run IT event in Indonesia. You should totally check it out! Here's my write-up of what we do, a bunch of lessons learned, and some publication covering our work.

Before all that, I did freelance graphic design, web design, and web development projects. I also enjoy making illustrations for fun.

I do other things too, like sketching buildings & statues I've been to/seen, traveling (& taking photographs whilst), writing fiction, and reading.
