— About

Hi! I'm Galuh.

I'm an undergraduate computer science student at Universitas Indonesia. Three of my favorite things are data, tech, & design.

Previously I worked as a data scientist intern in Midtrans, helping turn big data into usable data products where users can get various valuable insights from.

I also spent a year working on CompFest, the biggest student-run IT event in Indonesia. You should totally check it out! Here's my write-up of what we do, a bunch of lessons learned, and some publication covering our work.

Before all that, I did freelance graphic design, web design, and web development projects. I also enjoy making illustrations for fun.

I do other things too, like sketching buildings & statues I've been to/seen, traveling (& taking photographs whilst), writing fiction, and reading.
