— About

Hi! I'm Galuh.

Three of my favorite things are data, tech, & design. My passion is making data science and computer science more accessible to people.

Right now I am:

  • A data engineer at Midtrans
  • Participating in the Increasing Rust's Reach program. I'm working on Diesel, a safe, extensible ORM and Query Builder for Rust
  • Actively learning Rust--I document my notes here

Sometimes I:

  • Make fun side projects. I love mixing web development + data!
  • Share things I've learned by speaking at meetups and conferences
  • Translate open source projects and documentation from English to Bahasa Indonesia
  • Sketch buildings and read books

Previously I:

  • Studied computer science at Universitas Indonesia
  • Co-organized CompFest, the biggest student-run IT event in Indonesia. Here's my write-up of what we do, a bunch of lessons learned, and some publication covering our work
  • Did various freelance graphic design, web design, and web development projects
  • Made illustrations for fun

I can be reached through galuh.tunggadewi[at]gmail.com. I'm also on Twitter, Medium, and GitHub.
