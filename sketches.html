— Sketches

I love making sloppy sketches. I found it relaxing, & it sort of forces me to notice the details I otherwise wouldn't have noticed. Sometimes I paint too, but oftentimes laziness sets in. Sketching is simpler, & is good enough for me most of the time.

Tools of the trade Sakura Micron pens, Winsor & Newton watercolor sets, Canson papers.
